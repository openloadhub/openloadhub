# Code Of Conduct

OpenLoadHub follows the Contributor Covenant style of community conduct.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent, but accept corrections when impact differs from intent.
- Keep discussions focused on the project and its users.
- Give actionable feedback and avoid personal attacks.
- Respect maintainers' scope decisions for the public alpha.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, hate speech, or discriminatory language
- Sexualized language or imagery
- Publishing private information without explicit permission
- Repeated off-topic disruption
- Abusive demands for free support, private deployment work, or guaranteed response times

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove comments; close issues; block accounts; or restrict participation when behavior harms the project or community.

If you see a conduct issue, contact the maintainers through the repository moderation or private reporting channels. Do not include secrets or private customer data in public comments.
